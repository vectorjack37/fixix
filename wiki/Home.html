This is phun!

Monster truck simulation. See more screenshots in Media
Welcome to phunland.com, the official website of Phun! Phun is a free gamelike 2D physics sandbox where you can play with physics like never before. Children, students, engineers, artists, university professors, and many others, use Phun for telling stories, learning, constructing amazing machines, creating games - or just for the plain Phun of it.

Emil moves in with Algoryx

Emil is now a proud employee of Algoryx and Phun moves in with him. Algoryx is one of the major players in the market of interactive multiphysics. This is great news, because Emil now has access to the experience and competence at Algoryx, and can work full time on Phun together with other developers from Algoryx. One of the founders and CEO of Algoryx is Kenneth Bodin, who supervised Emil's MSc project in developing Phun. Phun will remain free, and Emil will still be closely involved with the community that made Phun what it is now.
